Summary: Difference among MVC, MVP and MVVM

This article is aimed to summarize three most popular design patterns: MVC, MVP, MVVM and weigh up the prons and cons among these three patterns.

MVC

MVC is the abbreviation of Model-View-Controller, which is commonly used for developing user interfaces that divides the related program logic into three interconnected elements. This is done to separate internal representations of information from the ways information is presented to and accepted from the user.
